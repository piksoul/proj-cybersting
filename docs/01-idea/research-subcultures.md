## üß¨ 6. How to Weave These Into the Novel (Gibson-style)

Gibson never paused exposition. He:
- referenced subcultures casually
- implied knowledge
- expected readers to catch up
- used slang as texture
- allowed mystery to stand

### Your equivalent:

- Elias passes through a **mining commune** using ex-military generators
- a **Spectacle influencer** becomes a villain
- a **Chaos Magick Discord** leaks the truth early
- a **DeFi nihilist** shows Elias how little anyone controls
- **urbex explorers** guide him through a physical labyrinth of abandoned agency data centres
- a **prompt artist** fakes intel that looks real
- **NPC streamers** parody the crisis live
- a **quant** explains the world with unhinged metaphors
- **post-ironic meme artists** turn the catastrophe into surreal humour

### The trick:
**never explain their culture ‚Äî let it exist in-scene.**
