- Can code (BTC, cryptography, AI) be a more "authentic" arbiter of truth than governments?

This intersects beautifully with the Tailor of Panama vibe.

### Why It Fits

Your entire plot includes:
- bureaucrats who don't understand the tools running the world,
- agencies working at cross-purposes,
- political panic,
- absurd overconfidence,
- cascading misinterpretations.

This is peak absurdism.

### Moments You Could Write

- A deadly chase sequence undermined by budget cuts in an intelligence division.
- A room full of economists arguing over a model that's wrong, but politically required.
- A realization that the Satoshi keys were "lost" due to a clerical error.
- A character who says, "We're not fighting each other. We're fighting entropy."

## üß© 3. Systems Theory / Complexity Theory (the secret backbone)

This is the secret philosophical underpinning of the entire novel.

### Why It Matters

- Bitcoin is a complex adaptive system.
- Nation-states are complexity machines.
- Markets are emergent organisms.
- Intelligence agencies are recursive feedback loops.
- AI is a system that studies systems.

### Concepts to Integrate Lightly

- Emergence (the network moves because the whole is smarter than its parts)
- Decentralisation as antifragility
- Monetary policy as a complex organism
- Game theory as ecological

**Beautiful, simple line to include:**
"The difference between control and chaos is just perspective."

- If money is trust, and trust is code, what is "self"?
- If a network knows more about you than you do, who owns your story?

These themes make your thriller human.
